Our Story

A Cut Above
Tradition

Founded in 2020, Crist & Crist was built on a single belief: every man deserves a great barber. What began as a father-and-son endeavor has quickly become a community cornerstone.

We believe a great barber doesn't just cut hair — he listens, observes, and elevates. Every man who sits in our chair leaves with more than he came in with.
