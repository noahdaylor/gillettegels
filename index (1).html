About Me

Hi, I'm Sophia — your nail artist

Welcome! I'm a certified nail technician with a passion for transforming nails into wearable art. Based locally, I offer a relaxing, one-on-one studio experience where your comfort and creativity always come first.

My journey began with a love for fine art — and I found my canvas in miniature. Each set I create is completely bespoke, designed around your personality, lifestyle, and vision.

I use only professional-grade, cruelty-free products and stay up to date with the latest techniques through ongoing education.
